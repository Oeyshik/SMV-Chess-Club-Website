Frequently Asked Questions

Who founded this club?

OEYSHIK DAS, a student of the Computer Science Department founded this club on his final year of the BSc.(Honours) Course.

When was this club established?

This club was established in 20th JULY, 2022 on International Chess Day.

Can I be a member of this club?

Yes, Ofcourse. Register yourself in the 'register' section of the website. WELCOME!

How much money do I have to pay to become a member?

It is free of cost.

How to participate in the events?

The details of the events is described in the 'Events' section of the website. Please visit.

Can students from different college participiate in the events?

The events are now primarily for the college faculties and students only. However we will try to accomodate willing members from outside the college in near future.
